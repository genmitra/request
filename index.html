About the Founder

Genmitra was founded by Mr. vivank singh from Hyderabad, a software engineer, with a vision to simplify access to essential government services for every Indian citizen. He believes in leveraging technology to bridge the gap between citizens and government processes, ensuring a seamless and transparent experience.

Mr. Singh's background in software engineering drove him to create a platform that empowers individuals to navigate complex procedures with ease.
